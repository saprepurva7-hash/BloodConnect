Why Donate Blood?

One blood donation can save up to three lives. BloodConnect connects donors with people in need quickly and efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Anyone who is healthy, between 18 and 65 years old, and meets the basic donation requirements.

How often can I donate blood?

Most healthy adults can donate whole blood every 3 months, depending on local guidelines.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes. Blood donation is completely safe. Sterile and disposable equipment is used for every donor.

How long does blood donation take?

The donation process usually takes about 10–15 minutes, while the complete visit takes around 30–45 minutes.

Can I donate blood if I have a cold or fever?

No. You should wait until you have fully recovered before donating blood.

Blood Donation Eligibility

✔ Age between 18 and 65 years

✔ Weight should be at least 50 kg

✔ Should be healthy on the day of donation

✔ No fever or active infection

✔ Last blood donation should be at least 3 months ago

✔ Carry a valid ID proof
